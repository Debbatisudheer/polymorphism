Polymorphism Explanation

Polymorphism is demonstrated through the calculateTotalPrice method. This method behaves differently depending on the subclass of the item passed to it. Each subclass (Electronics, Clothing, Book) may have its own implementation of the calculateTotalPrice method, allowing for different calculations to be performed based on the specific type of item.
